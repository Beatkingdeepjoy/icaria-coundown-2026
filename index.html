About ICARIA 2026

ICARIA is the annual cultural festival of ICFAI University Tripura, celebrating creativity, talent, and the vibrant spirit of our student community. Since its inception, ICARIA has grown to become one of the most anticipated events in the academic calendar.

This year, ICARIA 2026 promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with three days of non-stop entertainment, competitions, performances, and cultural activities. The festival brings together students from across the region to showcase their talents in music, dance, drama, art, and more.

Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with creativity, energy, and celebration!
